Who We Are

Pioneering Technical Solutions Since 1988

Roshani Technologies, founded in 1988 by Mr. Dipak Shah, stands as a leading provider of comprehensive consulting and training solutions. We specialize in areas such as engineering, procurement, construction project management, business process, Architecture development, product development, designing, Government sector, manufacturing sector, graphic design, and the entertainment sector.

Our commitment extends to enhancing productivity and streamlining processes for Design, analysis, and manufacturing through the effective implementation of IT systems. Over the years, we’ve been instrumental in catalyzing positive transformations for numerous businesses.

With a strong reputation, Roshani Technologies is the preferred partner for many, offering not just technical expertise but also financial and operational competitiveness. We remain dedicated to driving innovation and excellence in every facet of our service.
