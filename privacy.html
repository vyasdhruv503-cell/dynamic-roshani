Privacy Policy

At Roshani Technologies Pvt. Ltd., we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, enroll in our training programs, or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Residential Address (if required)
  • Date of Birth (where applicable)
  • Educational Qualification
  • Resume/CV
  • Government-issued ID (only when required for verification)
  • Payment Information (processed securely through authorized payment providers)

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Website Usage Data
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process course registrations.
  • Create and manage student accounts.
  • Provide training materials and certifications.
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests.
  • Send important notifications regarding classes, schedules, examinations, and certificates.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Process payments.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Prevent fraud and misuse of our services.

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality.
  • Remember user preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic.
  • Enhance user experience.

Users can disable cookies through their browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Payment gateway providers.
  • Government authorities when required by law.
  • Authorized service providers assisting in website hosting, communication, or technical support.
  • Certification partners (only where necessary).

All such parties are expected to protect your information appropriately.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Data loss
  • Misuse
  • Alteration
  • Disclosure
  • Destruction

Although we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide educational services.
  • Maintain student records.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Resolve disputes.
  • Enforce our agreements.

7. Student Responsibilities

Students are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate information.
  • Maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.
  • Informing us promptly if unauthorized account activity is detected.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party websites.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are generally intended for individuals aged 18 years and above.

If a minor enrolls in a course, parental or guardian consent may be required where applicable. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may request to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Update your details.
  • Request deletion of your information where legally permissible.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities.
  • Raise concerns regarding your personal data.

Requests will be processed within a reasonable time, subject to applicable legal requirements.

11. Communication

By registering with Roshani Technologies Pvt. Ltd., you consent to receive:

  • Admission updates
  • Course notifications
  • Examination announcements
  • Placement opportunities
  • Promotional communications

You may opt out of promotional communications at any time.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated Effective Date.

Continued use of our website or services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
